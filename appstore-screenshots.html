How to produce App Store screenshots (2880 x 1800):
1. Run Cabinet and take clean window screenshots: ⇧⌘4 then Space then click the window (hold ⌥ while clicking to skip the shadow).
2. Click or drag each screenshot into a slot below.
3. Edit any headline by clicking the text (everything is editable inline).
4. Capture each stage: ⇧⌘4 and drag exactly over the colored 1440 x 900 stage. On a Retina display that produces a 2880 x 1800 file, which is exactly what App Store Connect wants for Mac.
