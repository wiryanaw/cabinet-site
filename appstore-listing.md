## Description
Every important document, filed, tracked, and ready.

Cabinet is the calm, private home for the paperwork that runs your life. Visas, university applications, tax season, scholarships, work permits. Cabinet turns each one into a simple document checklist, keeps the originals in one place on your Mac, and tells you before anything expires.

No more "I swear I saved it somewhere." No more hunting through Downloads and email the night before a deadline.


KNOW EXACTLY WHAT IS MISSING
Create a process for any goal and list the documents it needs. Cabinet tracks what you have collected, what is still missing, and how close you are to done.

ONE FILE, EVERY PROCESS
Link a single passport to every application that needs it. Update it once and every checklist stays current. Cabinet warns you before you delete anything still in use.

EXPIRY DATES THAT ANNOUNCE THEMSELVES
Passports, test results, and permits expire quietly. Cabinet says it out loud, weeks before it matters.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
Everything lives on your Mac. There is no server, no account, and nothing to breach. Cabinet works fully offline and never uploads your files.

NEVER LOCKED IN
Export any process as a PDF checklist for your agent or consultant. Export your whole library as plain files and folders whenever you like.


FREE TO START
The free plan covers up to 15 documents and 3 active processes, forever.

CABINET PRO, ONE PAYMENT OF $15
Unlock unlimited documents and processes, full version history, expiry reminders with smart alerts, and PDF checklist export for any process. Pay once and own it forever. No subscription, ever.


Cabinet requires macOS 14 Sonoma or later.
