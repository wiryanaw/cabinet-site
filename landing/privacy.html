Privacy Policy

Cabinet is designed with privacy at its core.

Last updated 3 July 2026

Cabinet is a local document manager for macOS. Your documents never leave your Mac. There is no account to create, no server storing your files, and nothing for anyone to breach. This policy explains, plainly, exactly how Cabinet handles your data.

No data collection

Cabinet does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. Everything you add to Cabinet stays on your Mac. We have no access to your documents, file names, tags, or any other information you enter into the app.

Local storage only

All application data, including your documents, processes, and settings, is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Cabinet references your original files rather than copying them, so they remain exactly where you keep them on disk. Uninstalling Cabinet removes the app and its local database, and leaves your original files untouched.

No analytics or tracking

Cabinet contains no analytics SDKs, no crash reporters, and no third party tracking libraries. We do not track how you use the app, which features you open, or how often you launch it. There are no cookies, no advertising identifiers, and no profiles built about you.

Works offline

Cabinet is built to run fully offline and makes no background network requests. It never phones home. The only times the app touches the internet are the two optional actions described below, and only when you choose to trigger them.

Feedback you choose to send

If you use the Share Feedback feature inside the app, only the message you type and the category you pick are sent, so we can read and act on it. Your documents, file names, and personal data are never included. If you never use the feature, nothing is ever sent.

Calendar reminders you create

If you choose to add a document expiry to Google Calendar, Cabinet simply opens your browser with an event you have selected. Cabinet does not connect to your calendar or read anything from it. This only happens when you tap the button.

Purchases

If you buy Cabinet Pro, the transaction is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. Cabinet receives only the confirmation needed to unlock your license. We never see or store your payment card details, and there is no subscription to manage.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy or how Cabinet handles your data, email us at wiryanaw@gmail.com. We are happy to answer anything.

In one sentence

Cabinet keeps your documents on your Mac, collects nothing about you, and only touches the internet when you personally choose to send feedback, add a calendar reminder, or make a purchase.
